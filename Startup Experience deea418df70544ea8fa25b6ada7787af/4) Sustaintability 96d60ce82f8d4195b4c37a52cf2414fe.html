What’s impact in this context?

In the humanitarian & development sector, one of the main difficulty in measuring impact is the isolation of the
various factors influencing the person or phenomenon being impacted - Esther Duflo.
  • In Business, measuring a companies’ impact can take the form of various KPIs, often derived from Financial
    Data but not only, especially in a data driven economy (spend now, harvest profits later)
    • Turnover (Total; Per Product; Per Geographies; Per type (oneoffs/recuring) etc)
    • CoS (Total; Per Product; Per Geographies etc)
    • Profit (Total; Per Product; Per Geographies etc)
    • Unique Customers
    • Customers’ Lifetime
    • Upselling
