Customer Personas

  1. Creating customer personas may be useful to entrepreneurs because
    1. Helps in understanding segments and their deeper needs
    1. Creates user-centricity in product design
    1. Could enhance overall customer experience

  1. For our exercise, some aspects of these personas

    Demographics (B2C) / Firmographics (B2B):

    1. B2C: Age, gender, income level, education, occupation, location.
    1. B2B: Company size, industry, location, type of business, revenue.

    Goals and Motivations:

    1. B2C: Personal aspirations, lifestyle goals, what they strive to achieve through the product or service.
    1. B2B: Business objectives, what the company aims to achieve by using the product or service(e.g., increased efficiency, cost reduction).
