Stakeholders and Business Models

Questions to answer:

  1. Who will use our product or service? Who will pay?
  1. Who could partner with us?
  1. Who are our competitors?
  1. Who can advise or mentor us?
  1. Who do we have on the team?

Types of business models suitable for early-stage startups to consider

  • Online retail
  • Make to order or Direct sales
  • Subscription
  • Freemium
  • A combination of the above elements
